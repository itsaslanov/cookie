## About
At JOINUS KOREA, my role as a Full-stack Developer was to collaborate with my team to resolve issues and contribute to small-scale projects, honing my skills in Python and Source Code Control System. Our efforts were coordinated through regular Zoom meetings and Kakaotalk discussions, ensuring agile problem-solving and continual learning.

Previously, at Foreach.Soft Ltd, I focused on creating intuitive web interfaces using Figma and implementing them with Tailwind CSS, enhancing user experience. As a UI/UX Designer, my commitment to crafting seamless digital environments is grounded in my ongoing Business Administration studies at Pusan University of Foreign Studies, which shapes my strategic approach to management and operations within the tech space.
